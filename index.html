who we are

Alliance Select Foods International, Inc. (ASFII) is a proudly homegrown international seafood company established in 2003. As a leader in the industry, we are committed to producing high-quality products, including canned tuna, smoked salmon, and other seafood, with a focus on sustainability and global distribution. Recognized globally, we continue to deliver excellence while supporting our people, communities, and planet.
