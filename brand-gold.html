WHAT IS VENTRESCA?

  • THE “WAGYU” OF TUNA
    One of the tuna’s most sought-after cuts, admired for its rich, luxurious flavor
  • MELT-IN-YOUR-MOUTH TEXTURE
    Prized for its melt-in-the-mouth tenderness and sumptuous, full-bodied flavor.
  • TUNA BELLY CUT
    Derived from ventre, the Italian word for “belly”

THE MANUKA SMOKING DIFFERENCE

  • PURE NEW ZEALAND ORIGIN
    Smoked using wood chips from the Manuka tree – a native tree found only in New Zealand
  • ARTISANAL CRAFT, EXCEPTIONAL TASTE
    Cured with a special formulation that brings the exceptional, rich flavor perfect for seafood
  • NATURALLY RICH AROMA
    Its slow, aromatic burn delivers a warm, subtly sweet smokiness that elevates seafood to perfection
