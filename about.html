About

who we are

Alliance Select Foods International, Inc. (ASFII) is a homegrown international seafood company. It is a public corporation registered with the Philippine Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) on September 1, 2003, as Alliance Tuna International, Inc. The company began commercial operations focused on tuna processing, canning, and the export of canned tuna products in General Santos City, Mindanao, Philippines. In 2006, ASFII was listed on the Philippine Stock Exchange through an initial public offering and was renamed Alliance Select Foods International, Inc. in 2010.

what we do

Alliance Select Foods International, Inc. (ASFII) has been engaged in the research and development, manufacturing, processing, canning, importing, and exporting of marine food products and aquaculture for over 15 years.

Our main products are canned tuna and smoked salmon, including private label products for some of the world’s largest retailers.

tuna

Our tuna, caught in sustainable quantities in the teeming depths of the Pacific, is processed and canned in our very own canning facilities and distributed globally. They are rich in Omega-3 and are in high demand all over the world—used and trusted by popular food brands and stocked in the most well-known supermarket chains worldwide.

Salmon

Our salmon products are sourced from the deep seas of the Pacific and the Atlantic and smoked in the best facilities in the Philippines. We have invested in some of the most well-known names in the smoked salmon industry to provide you with products that are unparalleled in freshness, flavor, and price.
